CSO Poster Mailer

Typefaces: Didot, Avenir Next

Text Source: Colorado Symphony Orchestra 21-22 Season Schedule

Design Constraints: No more than two typefaces. Up to 3 Pantone Spot colors (plus black and paper color). You may use up to 3 Pantone Spot colors (plus black and the paper color). The design needs to be primarily typographic. Do not use photographs. Geometric shapes are allowed. One of the panels needs to contain mailing information (address, return address, placeholder for stamp).
